CEJA (Climate & Equitable Jobs Act) — workforce training (Illinois example)

CEJA is Illinois legislation focused on clean energy workforce development and equitable access to training and apprenticeships. If you are in Illinois, CEJA-funded programs provide free training, pre-apprenticeship pipelines, and supports for entry to trade careers in solar, HVAC, and other clean-energy trades.

Quick resources & how to apply

How to apply for grants & aid

  1. Complete the FAFSA: studentaid.gov
  2. Search federal funding: Grants.gov
  3. Contact your local workforce board for WIOA supports: dol.gov/wioa

Where to find scholarships & local help

  • Community colleges and trade schools — ask for financial aid & apprenticeship pathways
  • Unions and trade associations — apprenticeship scholarships and recruitment
  • State workforce agencies (search your state + "workforce board")
